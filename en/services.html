Ironing Service

Two options are available for you:


  1. If you want, you can bring your laundry to our establishment (at Rue de l'Alliance, 85, 1480 Clabecq) and it will be ready after 24 hours.
  2. If you have no means of locomotion, time or energy, we can also pick it up at your home and bring it back within 24 hours.

This option is valid if the quantity of clothes to be ironed is greater or equal to 2 hours of ironing.
