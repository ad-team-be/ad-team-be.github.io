How much does the housekeeping service costs?

In order to benefit from our services at home, it is necessary to be registered on Sodexo to be able to order the Paper or Electronic Service Titles.

An hour of service is equivalent to 1 Titre-Service (i.e., 9 €). On each Titre-Service.

You can also benefit from a tax reduction through a "refundable tax credit".

The refund request is made by attaching the tax certificate to the tax return to be submitted.

We will also take care to make your room tidy, vacuumed so that you can enjoy a restful sleep.

You can also call us to do your shopping, prepare your meal, washing and ironing your clothes, washing your windows and doing some other minor repairs.

For the independent mums

If you are a self-employed person, have had a child, and have resumed your professional activities after your maternity leave, you are entitled to 105 free service vouchers, granted through the social insurance funds for self-employed workers.

AD.TEAM will find you a service that suits you during this tiring period.

  • Paper Titres-services

    • Paper service vouchers are mailed in 3 business days after your order.

    You complete the service checks and give them to the housekeeper.

  • Electronic Titres-services

    • Titres-services vouchers are available immediately on your account.

    You do not give service checks to your housekeeper. It's up to her to encode her services via an automatic mail based on all instructions of his employer.

    After 5 working days, the approved company validates what has been encoded by the housekeeper.

    After the validation by the approved company, you have 4 working days to confirm or dispute the service (optional validation). After the period of 9 days, it is automatically validated without your intervention.

    On the other hand, if the service has been encoded by the approved company and not by your housekeeper (in the case you forgot the help of the housekeeper), there will be no automatic validation. The approved company will have to validate the service and the user will have to confirm the service via the Secure Space.
